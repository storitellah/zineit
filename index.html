Mini zine print

One sheet · eight panels · fold & cut — print it or save as PDF.

Print landscape at 100% scale, margins None, background graphics on. For PDF, choose Save as PDF in the print dialog. Photos keep their exact aspect ratios — the sheet scales uniformly.
Folding & cutting instructions
  1. Print the sheet landscape at 100% scale, margins set to none.
  2. Fold in half lengthwise (long edge to long edge) along the horizontal fold mark, then unfold.
  3. Fold in half widthwise, then fold each half back to the middle — the three vertical fold marks give you eight panels.
  4. Unfold to the lengthwise half-fold. Cut along the ✂ line only — the centre crease between the two middle panels.
  5. Open flat, fold lengthwise again, push the two ends toward the middle — the slit opens.
  6. Collapse into a booklet and crease with the front cover facing out.
